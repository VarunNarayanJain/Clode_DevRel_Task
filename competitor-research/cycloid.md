## Cycloid

- Website: https://www.cycloid.io
- Founder(s): Benjamin Brial
- Location: France
- Primary category: Platform Engineering / Internal Developer Platforms

### What they do
Cycloid is a platform engineering solution that helps organizations build internal developer platforms for managing cloud infrastructure and application deployments. It provides a self-service interface where developers can deploy and operate services without needing to manually handle underlying DevOps complexity.

### Target user (ICP)
Platform engineering teams, DevOps teams, and mid-to-large engineering organizations looking to standardize infrastructure workflows and improve developer self-service.

### Key features
- Internal developer portal for self-service deployments  
- GitOps-based infrastructure and application workflows  
- Environment and infrastructure templating  
- Governance, policy controls, and RBAC  
- FinOps and cost visibility integrations  

### Pricing
Not publicly disclosed (enterprise-focused offering).

### Positioning
Cycloid positions itself as a platform engineering tool that enables teams to build and operate Internal Developer Platforms, reducing friction between developers and infrastructure teams.

### Overlap with Clode
- Both aim to reduce manual DevOps effort through automation  
- Both focus on improving the deployment and operational phases of the SDLC  
- Both target platform and DevOps teams as key users  

### How Clode is different
- Cycloid primarily focuses on building an internal platform and self-service workflows, whereas Clode positions itself as an AI-powered automation layer across build, deploy, operate, and monitor stages  
- Clode emphasizes automation intelligence and reducing ongoing operational effort, rather than primarily providing a developer portal abstraction  
- Clode’s scope extends beyond standardized workflows into adaptive automation and monitoring

### DevRel angle
When talking about this space, Clode should emphasize that while internal platforms standardize workflows, many teams still spend significant time operating and maintaining those platforms. Clode can be positioned as reducing not just developer friction, but also long-term operational toil through deeper automation.
