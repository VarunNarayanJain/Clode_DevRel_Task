## Cerebrium

- Website: https://www.cerebrium.ai
- Founder(s): Michael Louis
- Location: Not publicly specified
- Primary category: Cloud Infrastructure

### What they do
Cerebrium is a cloud platform designed to deploy and run AI models in production with minimal infrastructure setup. It focuses on simplifying how teams serve machine learning models by handling provisioning, scaling, and runtime management behind the scenes.

### Target user (ICP)
Machine learning engineers, AI-focused startups, and engineering teams that need a simple way to deploy and operate ML models in production environments.

### Key features
- Managed deployment for AI and ML models  
- Automatic infrastructure provisioning and scaling  
- Serverless-style execution for inference workloads  
- Support for common ML frameworks and workflows  
- Focus on production readiness for AI systems  

### Pricing
Commercial SaaS offering (pricing not publicly listed).

### Positioning
Cerebrium positions itself as an infrastructure abstraction layer for AI workloads, allowing teams to deploy models without managing servers, scaling logic, or low-level cloud configuration.
