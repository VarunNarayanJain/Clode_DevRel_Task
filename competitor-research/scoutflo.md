## Scoutflo

- Website: https://scoutflo.com
- Founder(s): Kalpesh Bhalekar
- Location: India
- Primary category: AIOps / Observability

### What they do
Scoutflo is a DevOps productivity and operations platform that helps teams monitor workflows, track operational issues, and improve visibility across deployment and infrastructure processes. It focuses on giving engineering teams better insight into how their systems and DevOps pipelines behave in day-to-day operations.

### Target user (ICP)
DevOps engineers, SREs, and engineering managers who want better visibility into operational workflows and bottlenecks across infrastructure and deployments.

### Key features
- Visibility into DevOps and operational workflows  
- Monitoring and insights around deployments and incidents  
- Centralized view of operational metrics and activities  
- Focus on identifying inefficiencies and recurring issues  
- Lightweight integration with existing DevOps tools

### Positioning
Scoutflo positions itself as a DevOps productivity and operations visibility platform, helping teams understand and improve how infrastructure and deployment processes function over time.

### Overlap with Clode
- Both operate in the later stages of the SDLC, particularly deployment and operations  
- Both aim to reduce operational friction for DevOps and SRE teams  
- Both focus on improving reliability and efficiency in production environments  

### How Clode is different
- Scoutflo focuses on visibility and insights into operational workflows, whereas Clode focuses on automating those workflows  
- Scoutflo helps teams identify inefficiencies and issues, but remediation still requires manual action  
- Clode positions itself as reducing repeated operational work by automating common DevOps and infrastructure tasks  

### DevRel angle
When discussing tools like Scoutflo, Clode can emphasize that insight into operations is valuable, but teams often struggle with the manual effort required to act on those insights. Clode can be positioned as turning operational understanding into automated execution.
