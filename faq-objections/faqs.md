# FAQs and Common Objections

### 1. Is Clode just another DevOps tool?
No. Clode focuses on automating recurring deployment, infrastructure, and operational tasks, rather than adding another dashboard or manual workflow for teams to manage.

### 2. How is Clode different from internal developer platforms like Cycloid or Platformatic?
Internal platforms standardize how teams deploy and operate services. Clode focuses on reducing the ongoing operational work that still exists after those platforms are in place.

### 3. Does Clode replace observability tools like Groundcover?
No. Observability tools help teams understand what is happening in production. Clode focuses on automating the actions teams repeatedly take after issues are identified.

### 4. How is Clode different from analytics tools like LinearB?
LinearB helps teams measure and understand delivery performance. Clode focuses on automating the infrastructure and operational work that slows delivery down.

### 5. Is Clode meant only for large enterprises?
No. Clode is designed for teams that want to reduce manual DevOps effort as systems grow, whether they are scaling startups or larger engineering organizations.

### 6. How does Clode compare to DevOps consulting or services like InfraCloud?
DevOps services help teams design and adopt best practices. Clode focuses on productized automation that reduces long-term reliance on manual operations and external support.

### 7. Does Clode work only for AI or ML workloads like Cerebrium or ClearML?
No. Clode targets general cloud infrastructure and application operations, not just ML or AI-specific workloads.

### 8. Will using Clode reduce the need for DevOps or SRE teams?
Clode does not replace DevOps or SRE roles, but it helps teams spend less time on repetitive operational tasks and more time on higher-impact work.

### 9. How much setup and maintenance does Clode require?
Clode is designed to minimize ongoing maintenance by automating common workflows, reducing the operational overhead teams typically manage manually.

### 10. Is Clode another tool teams have to constantly configure?
No. Clode focuses on automation and execution rather than configuration-heavy workflows, helping teams reduce the number of tools they actively manage.
